## The Power of AI in Sprite Generation

Gone are the days of painstakingly crafting each frame of a character's animation by hand. With the `generateSprite` function, developers can now describe their desired character in plain text and let AI do the heavy lifting. This groundbreaking tool leverages DALL-E 3 to generate sprite sheets optimized for walking animations, complete with the perfect frame dimensions for engines like Phaser.js.

Imagine typing "a steampunk robot with glowing eyes" and receiving a fully-realized sprite sheet moments later. This not only saves time but opens up new realms of creativity, allowing for rapid prototyping and experimentation with character designs.

## Custom House Assets at Your Fingertips

Building immersive game worlds often requires a variety of unique structures. The `generateHouseAsset` function taps into the power of AI to create 2D house assets based on textual descriptions. Whether you need a "medieval stone cottage with a thatched roof" or a "futuristic skyscraper with neon accents," this function has you covered, generating assets that are ready to be dropped into your Phaser.js games.

## Advanced Image Processing Made Simple

Beyond asset generation, our SDK offers a range of powerful image processing functions:

- `removeBackgroundColor`: Easily remove specific background colors from images, perfect for creating transparent sprites.
- `getUniqueColors`: Extract color palettes from images, useful for maintaining visual consistency or creating dynamic color schemes.
- `encodeImage`: Convert images to Base64 strings, facilitating easy transmission and storage of image data.

These functions, powered by libraries like Jimp, make complex image manipulation tasks accessible to developers of all skill levels.
