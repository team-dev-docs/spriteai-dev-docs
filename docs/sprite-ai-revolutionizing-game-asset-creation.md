

  # Sprite AI: Revolutionizing Game Asset Creation

Welcome to the cutting-edge world of Sprite AI, where game development meets artificial intelligence! Our suite of tools is designed to streamline your asset creation process, making it faster, easier, and more creative than ever before.

## Unleash Your Creativity with AI-Powered Sprites

Gone are the days of painstakingly crafting each frame of your character animations. With our `generateSprite` function, you can bring your ideas to life in seconds. Simply describe your character, and watch as AI creates a fully-functional sprite sheet, complete with walking animations in a charming Super Nintendo style.

## Build Worlds with a Single Sentence

Imagine being able to populate your game world with unique buildings at the speed of thought. Our `generateHouseAsset` function does just that. From medieval cottages to futuristic skyscrapers, your architectural visions can be realized with a simple text description.

## Perfect Your Palette

Color is crucial in game design, and our `getUniqueColors` function gives you unprecedented control over your game's aesthetic. Analyze existing images to extract color palettes, ensuring consistency across your entire project.

## Seamless Integration

All our functions are designed with ease of use in mind. They integrate smoothly with popular game engines like Phaser.js, allowing you to focus on what really matters - creating an unforgettable gaming experience.
